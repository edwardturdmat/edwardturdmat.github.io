关于 About the Author 关于作者 About the Author

A brief introduction to the work that follows — and the threads that hold it together. 对接下来内容的简短引言，以及串联起这些内容的几条主线。

I am a final-year undergraduate at Fudan University's School of Social Development and Public Policy. My years at Fudan have been shaped by three threads I keep returning to: sociology coursework grounded in theory and method, hands-on research and fieldwork, and a parallel practice of writing and filmmaking for the university's public-facing channels.

I have served as independent researcher on a Xiyuan-funded study of non-teaching burdens carried by K–12 teachers in China, worked as a research assistant at the Fudan Development Institute on a comparative education project between Brazil and China, and contributed to the Center for American Studies' work on the U.S. Innovation Index. Alongside this, I produce video and editorial features for Fudan's official international accounts. I have placed in bilingual speech and intercultural-competence contests at the university level, and represented Fudan in a national short-film competition.

I tend to take on several things at once and see them through to the end. I work well under pressure, and the constant across my work — whether research, writing, or video — is the drive to do the task properly.

我是复旦大学社会发展与公共政策学院（School of Social Development and Public Policy）的大四本科生。在复旦的几年里，有三件事是我反复回到的：扎实的社会学理论与方法训练、田野调查与一手研究，以及为校方对外发布渠道进行的写作与影像创作。

我曾以独立研究者（Independent Researcher）身份主持复旦曦源项目，研究中国中小学教师的非教学负担问题；在复旦发展研究院（Fudan Development Institute）担任研究助理（Research Assistant），参与中国与巴西的比较教育项目；并为复旦大学美国研究中心（Center for American Studies）的「美国创新指数（USII）」项目贡献数据整理与报告撰写。除此之外，我长期为复旦官方国际传播账号制作视频、撰写英文专栏。我曾在复旦校内的双语演讲、跨文化能力等多项赛事中获得一等奖，并代表复旦参加全国大学生英语短视频比赛，获国赛银奖。

我习惯同时推进多项任务，并把每一件事做到收尾。我在高压环境下能保持稳定的产出节奏；无论是做研究、写作还是拍视频，「把事情认真做好」是我工作中始终不变的一根线。
